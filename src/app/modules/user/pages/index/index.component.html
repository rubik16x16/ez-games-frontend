ROADMAP

BETA OPENING DAY

Release our new EZ Battles 2.0 tournament platform along with our NFT token integration! Enjoy real time scoring, daily tournaments, custom wagers, crypto payouts, NFT integration and upgraded anti-cheat techniques. Players with our NFT, will gain exclusive access to daily tournaments.

Public release of platform

EZ Battles will go beyond beta an release to all public, with or without NFT. NFT Holders will be allowed exclusive tournaments, royalty benefits and upgraded customer service for their platform experience.

MONTHLY TALENT TOURNAMENTS

We will be inviting major players in the industry to participate in tournaments with our token holders. Once a month, our rare token holders (10% of tokens) will be able to participate in a tournament with their favorite streamers.

COMMUNITY INPUT

After our first 2 weeks live, we will host a twitter space with our partners to get community feedback on our platform and ultimately improve on our bugs, issues. We would like to host these at least once a month with token holders to continuously improve our platform.

EZ STORE

We are developing a fully loaded e-commerce site with our partners, to provide our players with all gaming supplies. From products like energy drinks to services like PC optimizations, you can find it at EZ Battles to improve your gaming.

TO BE CONTINUED

We aim to be the premier tournament platform for all gamers, not just for FPS gamers. We hope you follow us on this journey and help us improve, we are excited for what the future holds.
