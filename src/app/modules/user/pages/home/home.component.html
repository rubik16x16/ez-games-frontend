AM I ELEGIBLE TO COMPLETE IN EZ BATTLES TOURNAMENTS ?

Players around the world are welcome to compete in all free entry, credit prize EZ Battles tournaments.

To participate in tournaments with free entry and credit prizes: You must be 13 years or older.

To participate in tournaments with free entry and cash prizes: You must be 16 years or older and a legal resident of Australia, Austria, Belgium, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Denmark, Finland, Germany, Greece, Ireland, Mexico, Netherlands, Peru, Portugal, South Africa, Spain, Sweden, UK, or United States*

To participate in tournaments requiring entry fees:

You must be 18 years or older and a legal resident of Australia, Austria, Belgium, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Denmark, Finland, Germany, Greece, Ireland, Mexico, Netherlands, Peru, Portugal, South Africa, Spain, Sweden, UK, or United States*

*U.S. residents of Arkansas, Delaware, Michigan, and Tennessee are not currently eligible for cash prize tournaments.
